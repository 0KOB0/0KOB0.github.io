Meaning NewJeans

NewJeans is a double-meaning name. Firstly, jeans as a piece of garment are known for their timelessness. Each generation has its emblematic new style of jeans. This group follows the same ambition to become a representative figure of this generation’s pop culture.

OMG

Jan 2, 2023

Almost everyone has heard of the viral song OMG. The song's popularity propelled NewJeans into global fame, with it being used in numerous celebrity edits across various social media platforms. This song expresses NewJeans' unique and trendy tone in various styles, and it is expected that you will be able to feel another personality and charm of NewJeans. It dominated the iTunes Top Songs charts in nine regions, including Singapore, Thailand, and Vietnam.

DITTO

Dec 19, 2022

Ditto secured the rookie group's inaugural perfect all-kill on December 26, 2022, becoming the seventh song that year to attain this accomplishment. The song has overtaken the group's previous hits Hypeboy and Attention to reach new heights on the Billboard music charts. During the week of January 5, 2023, the song reached a career peak for the quintet by entering the Top 10 Hits on both Billboard's Global 200 and Global Excluding U.S. charts.

HYPE BOY

Aug 1, 2022

NewJeans caught global fans' attention with their debut music video and song. Their latest release, “Hype Boy,” has garnered even more attention due to its diverse representation and unique concept. Making it at no. 3 in our list of Best Songs of NewJeans Ranked. Hype Boy features music videos centered around each member, with unconventional love interests that challenge stereotypes. As they strive to become a global girl group, Min Hee-jin‘s (NewJeans CEO) band is making all the right moves to capture international interest.
